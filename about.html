About S & T Barber Lounge

Welcome to S & T Barber Lounge & Accessories – your premier destination for premium grooming in Chilanga, Lusaka West. We blend traditional barbering techniques with modern trends to give every client a fresh, confident look.

Our lounge is more than just a haircut – it's an experience. We offer a full range of services: skin fades, beard sculpting, hot towel shaves, and kids' cuts. We also stock authentic grooming accessories (oils, pomades, clippers, and more) so you can maintain your style at home.

Founded with a passion for excellence, our team of skilled barbers ensures every visit leaves you looking and feeling your best. Walk-ins are always welcome, but we recommend booking via WhatsApp for priority service.
